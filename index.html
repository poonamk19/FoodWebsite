Food Ordering

An online food ordering system is software that allows restaurant businesses to accept and manage orders placed via a website or mobile app. The process consists of a customer choosing the restaurant of their choice, scanning the menu items, choosing an item, and finally choosing for pick-up or delivery. Payment is then administered by paying with a credit card or debit card through the app or website or in cash at the restaurant when going to pickup
